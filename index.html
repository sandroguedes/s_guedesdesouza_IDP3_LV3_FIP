ABOUT CONTACT

So, what is it all about?

I cannot recall a time when I wasn't, one way or another, obsessed with beauty in all it's forms. Above all else, though, it's the presentation of great visuals that ended up becoming my main focus. Shape, form, color and formatting have always fascinated me, and that's increasingly true now that I have years of designing experience under my belt, both as a student and as professional.


If you would like to know more about the artist behind the gallery items in this site, click or tap on the buttom below to check out my "about" section.
